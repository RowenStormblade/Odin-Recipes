Shrimp Taco Bowls

This recipe is a healthy meal that is great for meal prepping!

  • 20 Shrimp
  • 1 tablespoon Olive Oil
  • 1 Clove Garlic
  • 1/2 teaspoon Ground Cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon Chili Powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon Onion Powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon Kosher Salt
  • 2 cups Brown Rice
  • 1 cup Black Beans
  • 1 cup Corn
  • 1 cup Tomato
  • 1/2 cup Cheddar Cheese
  • 2 tablespoons Cilantro
  • 1 Lime

    • To cook the Shrimp:

    1. In a medium bowl whisk together the olive oil, garlic, cumin, chili, and onion powders. Cover and refrigerate for at least 10 minutes.
    2. After, add in peeled and deveined shrip to mix and toss to coat completely.
    3. Cover and refrigerate for at least 10 minutes or up to 24 hours.
    4. Heat an iron skillet on high for up to 2 minutes. Add olive oil and shrimp. Turn heat down to medium.
    5. Cook shrimp on medium heat until pink and cooked through. About 5 minutes.

    To assemble:

    1. Cook the brown rice according to package directions.
    2. Put the rice into a bowl and cover with shrimp. Top with lime juice, salsa, sour-cream, or gucamole if you want!

    I hope you enjoy your meal!

Korean Chicken Thighs

This delicious chicken is sure to be a dinner hit! This recipe is for four servings.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 pounds Chicken Thighs
  • 1 tablespoon Cooking Oil
  • Sesame Seeds
  • Scallions

Marinade Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons Low Sodium Soy Sauce
  • 1 tablespoon Rice Vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons Gochujang Paste
  • 2 tablespoons Light Brown Sugar
  • 1 teaspoon Ginger Powder
  • 1 teaspoon Black Pepper
  • 1 tablespoon Minced Garlic

Steps to make!

  1. Clean and pat chicken thighs dry.
  2. In a mixing bowl create the marinade. To do this, combine soy sauce, pice vinegar, gochujang paste, brown sugar, ginger powder, black pepper, and minced garlic.
  3. Fully coat chicken thighs in marinage and let sit for at least 2 hours.
  4. Heat up a skillet and add in a tablespoon of cooking oil. Cook chicken thighs for about 3-5 mintues per side or until done.

The last step is to enjoy your meal!

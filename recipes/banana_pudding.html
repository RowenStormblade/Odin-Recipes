Banana Pudding

This dessert will be a fantastic end to any night!

Ingredients

  • 25 Golden Oreos
  • 6 tablespoons Butter
  • 8 ounces Cream Cheese
  • 1/4 cup Granulated Sugar
  • 8 ounces Whipped Topping
  • 3.4 ounces Banana Cream Pudding Mix
  • 1 cup Milk

The process!

  1. Crush up the 25 oreos and mix them with the butter. Press into the bottom of a 8x8" square pan. Chill for 10 minutes.
  2. Beat cream cheese and sugar in a large bowl until smooth. Stir in 1 cup of the whipped topping until combined, then spread over pan.
  3. Add the pudding mix to a large bowl (you can use the same one you used before). Whisk in milk until pudding thickens. Once it has set, spread it over the cream cheese layer.
  4. Top with the remaining whipped cream and some additonal crushed oreos for decoration.
  5. Chill for at least 2 hours before slicing and serving.

Optional: add 1-2 sliced bananas between the cream cheese and pudding layer!

That's the recipe! Enjoy!
